Lamborghini Revuelto

Powertrain and Performance

At the heart of the Revuelto is a new, naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine that produces 814 horsepower on its own. This engine is paired with three electric motors—two on the front axle and one integrated into the new 8-speed dual-clutch transmission—which boost the total power output to a staggering 1001 horsepower. This makes the Revuelto the most powerful Lamborghini ever produced.

  • 0-62 mph (0-100 km/h): 2.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: Over 217 mph (>350 km/h)

Design and Aerodynamics

The Revuelto's design is both a nod to Lamborghini's heritage and a look toward the future. It maintains the iconic wedge shape and scissor doors of its predecessors but introduces a new design language with Y-shaped headlights and hexagonal design elements throughout. The V12 engine remains a centerpiece, fully displayed at the rear of the car.

Chassis and Technology

The Revuelto is built on a new, all-carbon fiber chassis that is lighter and stiffer than the Aventador's. This contributes to its agile handling, which is further enhanced by a rear-wheel steering system and electric torque vectoring. The interior is designed to feel like a pilot's cockpit, with a focus on the driver. It features three screens: a 12.3-inch instrument cluster, an 8.4-inch central infotainment display, and a 9.1-inch screen for the passenger.
